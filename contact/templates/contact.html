Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions. Can't find what you're looking for? Contact us using the form above.

How do I borrow a book?

Simply search for the book, click "Borrow," and it will be added to your account. You can manage all your borrowed books in the "My Books" section.

What's the borrowing period?

Books can be borrowed for 7 days by default. You can renew books up to 2 times if no one else has reserved them.

Is there a mobile app?

Currently, our website is fully responsive and works great on all devices. A dedicated mobile app is coming soon!

Are there any fees?

Our basic library service is completely free. Late fees may apply if books are returned after the due date.

Can I request new books?

Yes! Use our contact form to request new books. We review all requests and add popular titles to our collection regularly.

Is my data secure?

Absolutely. We use industry-standard encryption and security measures to protect your personal information and reading history.
